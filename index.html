Deepak's Special Trip wali Maggi

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack of Deepak wali special maggie
  • 2 chopped onions
  • 1 tomato
  • Ghee 4 tbs
  • Garlic, Ginger, and my very fav paneer

Method to prepare:

  1. Take the ghee in pan
  2. Heat it and add the cut vegetables
  3. Cook them for some while and add water
  4. Add Masala
  5. Cook for some while
  6. Add noodles and cook until the water is absorbed
  7. Add raw paneer to it (Deepak's speciality)
  8. Serve hot and enjoyiiii!
